About Me

Self-Taught Software Developer

During the start of 2020, I was introduced to a simple app by Google called Grasshopper to study code. I became intrigued with it so I searched more on coding and how I could apply it to my life. I had this prejudice that it would be too difficult to learn until a video on YouTube with many influential people said anyone could code. So I believed it and chose a career path to continue. I have found apps such as Codecademy, Programming hub, Mimo, and Treehouse to learn how to code. This webpage is one of the many projects I've constructed to hone in on my skills. I've been doing projects in HTML, CSS, JS, and Python for months so feel free to check them out.
